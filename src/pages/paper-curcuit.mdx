# Opdracht | Paper circuit
Materials:

- Copper Tape: Used to create conductive paths on paper.
- LEDs (Light Emitting Diodes): Emit light when electricity flows through them.
- Coin Cell Batteries (3V): Compact power sources suitable for paper circuits.
- Paper or Cardstock: Serves as the base for the circuit.
- Anything else you can think of!
Create Your Own Illuminated Design:

Design and build a paper circuit, make a paper artwork out of it! You can use the different machines we have at the MakersLab, maybe to cut the shapes of the paper into complex forms or the viny cutter, the UV and RISO printer for fun colors and designs.
The paper circuit can be 3D or 2D, have fun experimenting with shapes, forms and colors. 
The Tinker Moments

Find those tinker moments. which consist of:

- What was your process step?
- What went 'wrong' (= unexpected outcome)
- What inspiration do you get from this mistake?
- Show how you apply this inspiration in your work.
