# Opdracht
You can use any of the different machines we have:

- Laser Cutter: To craft texture stamps and carving tools from materials like acrylic or wood.
- Vinyl Cutter: To create adhesive stencils for precise sgraffito designs.
- 3D Printer: To produce custom stamps and intricate texture plates.
- Foam cutter: To create stamps
- Carpentry tools
- Creating a ceramic piece with air dry clay:

To prepare:

- Knead the clay to remove air bubbles.
- Roll it to your desired thickness.
- Give it shape
- Apply one (or more of) the techniques you want to explore, like sgraffito or slab building
- Apply an even layer of paint
